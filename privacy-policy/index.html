1 Who we are

3 What data is collected and how it is protected

We operate a hash-first, no-plaintext architecture. Before any data leaves your device, phone numbers are transformed into an irreversible fingerprint using HMAC-SHA256 with a static salt bundled inside the app binary. This means:

  • The original phone number is never transmitted.
  • The hash cannot be reversed to obtain the original number.
  • The salt is bundled in the app â€” hashing works offline on first launch.

4 What we store on our servers

Our backend (Supabase, hosted in the EU) holds only the following:

  • Reputation records: keyed by the HMAC-SHA256 hash of a number â€” report count, unique reporter count, confidence score, spam category, and timestamps. No real number is stored.
  • Report events: an append-only log of spam reports â€” hash, device token hash, category, timestamp. Used to compute confidence scores and detect abuse.
  • Reporter deduplication table: (number hash, device token hash) pairs that ensure one device contributes one report per number. No real number or personal data.
  • Family pairing records: a hashed pairing token and an expiry timestamp. Automatically deleted 10 minutes after creation or when you unpair.

Records with zero reports for 12 months are automatically purged. We do not store IP addresses in association with any number hash.

5 How we use the data

We use the data collected for the following purposes only:

  • Spam detection: look up a number hash against the reputation database to compute a spam confidence score and decide whether to block, silence, or allow a call.
  • Reputation improvement: aggregate anonymous spam reports to improve confidence scores for all users.
  • Abuse prevention: use device token hashes to rate-limit reports and prevent coordinated manipulation of the reputation database.
  • Family Protection sync: relay blocking rules between a guardian device and up to one dependent device using hashed pairing tokens.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating and improving the call-screening service described above.

6 What we never do

  • We do not access your contacts list.
  • We do not read your call log.
  • We do not record or listen to call audio.
  • We do not transmit real phone numbers to any server.

  • We do not display ads.
  • We do not track your location.
